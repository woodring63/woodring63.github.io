Leadership

Geoffroy Hall Community Advisor

Starting in January 2017, I began work as a Community Advisor in the new Geoffroy Hall residence hall. My responsibilities include operating the social media sites, providing all variety of information to residents, working the hall desk, being on-call in a rotating schedule, holding regular meeting with the hall directors and peers, and creating bulletin boards and door decorations.

First Year Honors Program Leader

In the Fall of 2014, I was a leader for the First Year Honors Program (FHP). I was paired with a co-leader and together, we planned activities, brought in speakers from around campus, and went on tours of useful buildings on campus. However, the most important thing we did was create an environment in which we could help prepare freshman honors students for college life. The students were able to interact with twelve other freshman honors students from a variety of majors and, through that, form friendships, all while learning how to be succesful in college.
