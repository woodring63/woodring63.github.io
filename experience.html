Work Experience

Higher Learning Technologies

During the summer of 2016, I had an internship at Higher Learning Technologies in Coralville, Iowa. While there, I primarily worked on the iOS team, but spent a little bit of time working with the Android and Web development teams.

One of the major things I worked on while there was adding answer hiding to their flashcards. If a user wished to hide an answer was incorrect, they were now presented with a button which toggles the answer off and grays out the selection. Another major project of mine was optimizing the build process. I wrote a couple of scripts to better take advantage of "BuddyBuild", the software they used to build and deploy apps to the app store. Furthermore, I was had to fix some failing tests to optimize this build process.

At the end of the internship, I gave a presentation to my managers about what I had accomplished over the course of the summer.

During this experience, I not only learned technical information such as Swift, Objective C, and BuddyBuild, but also how to work on an established code base and on a large team. I was able to experience first hand what it is like to work in agile environment at a startup.
